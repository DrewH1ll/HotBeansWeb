About

Hot Beans Web is a company dedicated to designing and maintaining websites. Here at Hot Beans Web we can help you create your very own website for any purpose, it could be for a business or for personal use such as a blog. We offer a wide variety of options when creating a website from things such as choosing which language to code the website in, allowing you to provide any videos, images etc you wish to be integrated into the website. We will also test the website of all aspects before it is put up aswell as continuing to fix any bugs or problems that may occur in the future in a timely manner.

Hot Beans Web is a company that has been active since 2014 and has been a successful business and has made and been maintaining hundreds of websites for the past 10 years. We started off as a small company with very few employees but with time we have grown bigger and bigger with hundreds of employees. All our employees are hard working and thrive as a team to get work done as soon as possible. To be an employee/trainee here you are required to have: at the minimum a bachelors degree in computer science or any related experiences/courses. Keen knowledge and ability in website programming languages (JavaScript, HTML, SQL, etc), A good understanding in website design principles. Having a portfolio of any web development projects or certifications isnt required but may assist in your application/employment process.
